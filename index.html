Full stack Developer

02-02-2020

Becoming a full stack web developer is not a state of being. It's a process. Code Schools and online courses want to sell this, frankly bullshit, idea that you can study for 12 weeks or a year and boom you are a Full Stack developer. It's wrong headed. At that point you will have the basic skills of a stack of development concepts that make an app. It's Full Stack Light at best, but more likely just marketing. Becoming truly full stack will involve learning that never quite ends. A commitment to understanding just a bit more at the fringe of what you currently know and then repeating.
